Nacio de una necesidad real: ayudar a registrar ventas de forma clara, sin depender de servidores externos ni configuraciones complejas. Con Cosecha puedes organizar tus productos, registrar ventas en segundos y entender el rendimiento de tu negocio con reportes utiles.

Funciones principales:
- Gestion de productos con imagen local.
- Registro rapido de ventas con historial.
- Panel de inicio personalizable con widgets y presets.
- Panel de reportes personalizable con widgets y presets.
- Filtros avanzados de reportes (canal, producto, rango de monto y cantidad minima).
- Vista de rendimiento con tendencias, heatmap y barras mensuales.
- Exportacion a Excel configurable (modelos y campos).
- Almacenamiento local en el dispositivo.
- Respaldos opcionales con cifrado.
- Perfil de negocio con preferencia de moneda.
- Soporte multilenguaje (espanol e ingles).

Cosecha esta pensada para acompanar tu flujo de trabajo real: directa, practica y enfocada en vender mejor.

## English (EN)

### App Name (App Store / Play Store, max 30)
Cosecha: Sales & Inventory

### Subtitle (App Store, max 30)
Fast sales and inventory

### Short Description (Play Store, max 80)
Track products, sales, and reports for your business in seconds.

### Full Description
Cosecha is a simple, fast sales tracker built for small businesses that need clear day-to-day control.

It was created from a real-world need: recording sales quickly and reliably without external servers or complex setup. With Cosecha, you can organize products, log sales in seconds, and understand business performance through practical reports.

Key features:
- Product management with local images.
- Fast sales tracking with history.
- Customizable Home dashboard with widgets and presets.
- Customizable Reports dashboard with widgets and presets.
- Advanced report filters (channel, product, amount range, minimum quantity).
- Performance views with trends, heatmap, and monthly bars.
- Configurable Excel export (models and fields).
- Local-first on-device storage.
- Optional encrypted backups.
- Business profile with currency preferences.
- Multi-language support (Spanish and English).

Cosecha is designed for real selling workflows: clear, practical, and focused on helping you run your business better.
